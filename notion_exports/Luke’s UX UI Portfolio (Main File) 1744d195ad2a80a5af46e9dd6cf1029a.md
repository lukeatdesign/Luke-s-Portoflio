# **Hey, I’m Luke 🥰💖**

**As a UX/UI Designer, I have extensive experience across various projects, specializing in tooling applications and enhancing designer skills.**

> Hi, I'm Lookchin (or you can call me Luke), a Computer Engineering graduate who transitioned into UX/UI Design, I've accumulated **7 years of experience** with successful product launches through working on design **full-loop from research, define, ideate, prototyping, and testing.**
> 

I have extensive experience in **leadership roles**, including serving as **UX Lead at Alles Labs** where I ensured **design quality, mentored designers, and established team standards.**

I've also been deeply involved in **hiring processes**, from creating job descriptions to interviewing candidates, helping to build **strong teams and support career growth.**

I've written **several articles** about growing as a UX/UI Designer that have been **shared over 1,000 times.** These works led to **speaking opportunities** at various events, where I became known for my **unconventional and practical** approach. I'm also working as an instructor, **teaching courses** about the academic side of UX/UI design.

**I recently worked at Initia Labs,** which I’d been working with following the **acquisition of our startup,** founded in 2022. **As a member of founding-team,** we launched **Celatone**, a smart contract explorer and developer's portal for on-chain smart contract development in the Cosmos ecosystem.
