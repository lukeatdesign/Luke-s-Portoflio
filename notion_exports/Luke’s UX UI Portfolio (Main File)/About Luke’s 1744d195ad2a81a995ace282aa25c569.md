### **Forcing Myself Into Having a Reading Habit**

I’m not a born-to-be reader. And I found myself not having such good grades when I was a student. In despair of my own futures, I try other ways to gain knowledge outside the classrooms.

Fortunately, many introduce me to of many soft and hard skills and sharpen my attitudes. In case you are interested, you can see more about my reading list in [**my Goodreads profile**](https://www.goodreads.com/user/show/85462937-lookchin).

### **I Was The One Who Dare To Dream.**

When I was younger, I was aware that I would not make a good software engineer myself and dreamed of sports media-related jobs. I founded a Facebook page about Spanish Football (LaLiga) with more than 180,000 followers, which led me to being offered the position of **a Digital Strategy Director at Khobsanam**, one of the most well-known sports media organization in Thailand.

During my time as a content creator, I received many opportunities to be an event MC, a football commentator, and an eSport commentator.

### **Strong Personalities With Many Soft Sides**

I genuinely believe that strong **leadership comes with radical empathy**. I always try to improve myself to be an active listener who speaks out clear ideas.

I consider myself just an ordinary person who is still far from perfection and would never be but at least I try my best to get closer to that word every day.

Beyond work, I spend time reading some books, playing Street Fighter 6, and taking time with my partner and our cat, Meonji.
